Detailed Description

Since she was a child, Ligia loved to play video games of any genre. Because of this, she got a skill in addapting with any type of user interface. In general school she begins to like informatic fields because of her kins, not much older than her, who are learning in computer science related fields.
Nowadays, in highschool, Ligia is looking for a problem solving app to learn an useful programming language. From her teachers finds out about INFOX.
Because of her past in gaming, Ligia get easly familiar with the UI. Problems requirements are very clear because these are verified by admin and the information architecture is good. The things she doesn't like are that there are too many chapters in the list and get easly lost and problem hints must be bought with "app currency", which create some sort of fear of not having coins. She want to give feedback to an admin about all this but app infrastructure does not allow it.

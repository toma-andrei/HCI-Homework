Detailed Description

Marius is a student at the Faculty of Psychology and Education. He choosed this field because he wanted a bachelor's degree but with a medium amount of work. His parents are not happy with his choice because this field is not suitable for him. After one year and a half of faculty he agreed with his parents and tried to to find a new study field. He heard from friends that computer science is a very well paid field. From internet finds out about INFOX and immediately got on the app. For the first touch he just wanted to see how the problems are like but the only thing he could do was to create an account. Marius created the account and after a few seconds he was able to see the app. First he got to his profile page and updated his account. Because of history in video games student got used immediately with the interface. Because of the multitude of chapters and problems, Marius got really confused but finally managed to choose a problem. The problem was a very easy one but he was not able to solve it because of his poor computer science knowledge.
Because of low determination and lack of a good mentor, the boy got descuraged and left the app, looking for other field to study.
